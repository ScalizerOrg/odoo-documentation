==============
Extra features
==============

This section covers features common to both :doc:`retail <shop>` and :doc:`restaurant
<restaurant>` settings, including employee management, pricing rules, and self-ordering.

.. cards::

   .. card:: Multi-employee management
      :target: extra/employee_login
      :large:

      Allow multiple employees to log into a single POS register with specific roles.

   .. card:: Preparation display
      :target: extra/preparation
      :large:

      Use dedicated screens to track order preparation stages, and inform customers when their
      order is ready for pickup.

   .. card:: Self-ordering
      :target: extra/self_order
      :large:

      Set up self-ordering to allow customers to place orders via QR codes on their own devices
      or through a dedicated kiosk.

   .. card:: Presets
      :target: extra/presets
      :large:

      Apply predefined configurations to orders, such as specific pricelists, taxes, or return
      modes.

   .. card:: Pricing features
      :target: extra/pricing
      :large:

      Configure advanced pricing rules including manual or automatic discounts, loyalty programs,
      cash rounding, pricelists, and fiscal positions.
